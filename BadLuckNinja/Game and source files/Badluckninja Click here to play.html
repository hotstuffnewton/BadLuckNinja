Bad Luck Ninja

About

Geoff is the world's worst ninja.This is not because he lacks the ability to sneak around stealing children's sweets but because he's so unlucky. Guide Geoff through the level safely so that he may assassinate his planned and paid for target, be mindful of your surroundings Geoff's bad luck may become your down fall.

Game Play

-A/D/W - run left or right and jump
-Dying will result in a respawn from the last check point
-Gain points for completing the level quickly
-During boss fights you must dodge for the set time to win.
